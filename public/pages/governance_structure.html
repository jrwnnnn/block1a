Governance Structure

Section 1. The Council

3.1. The Council shall serve as the primary governing body of the server, responsible for enforcing rules, resolving disputes, and overseeing player rights as outlined in this Constitution.

3.2. The Council shall possess the authority to:

  • Conduct CoreProtect inspections upon request;
  • Manage disputes concerning territorial claims, griefing, and rule violations;
  • Oversee and facilitate the amendment process for constitutional modifications;
  • Implement and enforce disciplinary actions as prescribed in Article VI.

    • 3.3. The Council shall operate with transparency and impartiality, ensuring that all decisions are made in accordance with server laws and principles of fairness.

    Section 2. The Overseer

    3.4. The Overseer shall hold the highest executive authority within the server, possessing the ultimate power to approve or veto major server decisions, rollbacks, and constitutional amendments.

    3.5. The Overseer shall function as the final arbiter in high-stakes disputes, ensuring that critical decisions are made with full consideration of server-wide impact.
