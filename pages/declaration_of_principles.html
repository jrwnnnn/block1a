DECLARATION OF PRINCIPLES

Section 1. Supremacy of the Constitution

1.1. This Constitution shall be recognized as the highest governing authority of the CS1A Minecraft Server. All server rules, policies, and decisions must be in alignment with its provisions, and any conflict shall be resolved in favor of this document.

1.2. No rule, policy, or regulation shall supersede, contravene, or otherwise diminish the authority of this Constitution unless formally amended in accordance with the procedures outlined herein.

1.3. &rThe Council, as the designated governing body, shall bear the responsibility of ensuring strict adherence to this Constitution and the enforcement of its provisions.
