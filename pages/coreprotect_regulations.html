COREPROTECT REGULATIONS

Section 1. CoreProtect Lookup Rights

6.1. Players may submit a request for CoreProtect log inspections in cases of suspected rule violations.

6.2. The Council shall be obligated to review and act upon lookup requests within 48 hours.

Section 2. CoreProtect Rollback Policy

6.3. Rollbacks shall be strictly regulated and require direct approval from the Overseer.

6.4. Rollbacks shall be granted only in cases of severe griefing, large-scale destruction, or hacking-related incidents, following a formal review process.

6.5. Before approving a rollback, the Council must:

  • Verify the extent and severity of the damage;
  • Ensure that a rollback would not negatively affect other players
  • Present all findings to the Overseer for final determination
