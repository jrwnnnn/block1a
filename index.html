About

Melo is here to help you get in touch with your emotions and make sense of how you’re feeling, one day at a time. Life can be busy and sometimes it’s hard to pause and notice what’s going on inside. Melo gives you a simple way to reflect on your mood, so you can better understand yourself and the things that matter to you. It’s about taking small moments to check in with yourself, find peace in your feelings, and remember that it’s okay to feel however you do.
